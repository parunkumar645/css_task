Creative Web Layout

Welcome to this creative web layout! In this article, we explore the use of various pseudo-elements in CSS to enhance the visual appeal of our webpage.

CSS pseudo-elements like ::first-line, ::first-letter, ::before, ::after, ::marker, and ::selection provide us with the flexibility to style specific parts of our content in unique ways.

Let's make our web pages more engaging and visually interesting by leveraging the power of CSS pseudo-elements. Happy coding!
